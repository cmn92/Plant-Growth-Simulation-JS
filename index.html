Plant Growth Game

About the Plants

In this simulation, three unique plants each have special strengths. They race to gather sunlight and water, while dodging hazards. Cheer on your choice as they struggle for survival!

Plant 1: Extremely fast growth, but average resilience and efficiency.

Plant 2: Tough and resilient, hazards barely faze it, though it's slower.

Plant 3: Highly efficient at using resources, thrives with less input.

About Resources

The plants compete for two essential resources:

Sunlight: Abundant in upper regions. Greatly boosts plant health.

Water: Located lower down. Provides moderate but steady health gains.

Collecting these resources replenishes health and allows plants to survive longer.

About Hazards

Not everything is easy for the plants. Various threats lurk in the field:

Biotic Stress (Moving Hazard): Drifts around the field, harming plants that get too close.

Environmental Stress (Static Hazard): Stationary but dangerous. Getting near it drains plant health.

Some plants are more resilient and lose less health when encountering these threats. Choose wisely!
