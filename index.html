About the Plants

In this simulation, three unique plants each have special strengths. They race to gather sunlight and water, while dodging hazards. Cheer on your choice as they struggle for survival!

Plant 1: Extremely fast growth, but average resilience and efficiency.

Plant 2: Tough and resilient, hazards barely faze it, though it's slower.

Plant 3: Highly efficient at using resources, thrives with less input.
